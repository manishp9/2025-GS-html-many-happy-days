Connections is an exclusive daily program where heimish women aged 60 and above gather for activities that provide friendship, fulfillment, and fun.

The social circle for the class of 1980

At Connections, like-minded women meet daily to make new friends and reconnect with past acquaintances. Our program is filled with intellectual stimulation, artistic endeavors, and dynamic fitness.

Our carefully curated schedule is created with the interests of women in mind and is packed with a variety of engaging, refreshing activities.
